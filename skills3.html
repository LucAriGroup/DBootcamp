Behavioral Issues We Address

Our program is designed to target specific behavioral challenges with proven intervention strategies

Laziness & Lack of Motivation

Structured routines, physical activities, and goal-setting exercises help participants develop discipline and find purpose.

Bad Attitude & Disrespect

Communication skills training, respect workshops, and emotional intelligence development.

Drug & Alcohol Abuse

Specialized substance abuse program with counseling, support groups, and healthy habit formation.

Back Chatting & Defiance

Authority respect training, conflict resolution skills, and consequence understanding.

Low Self-Confidence

Confidence-building activities, achievement recognition, and positive reinforcement techniques.

Peer Pressure Challenges

Assertiveness training, values clarification, and resistance skills development.

Leadership Skills Development

Team-building exercises, responsibility assignments, and leadership opportunity creation.

Skills Development Programs

Practical skills training that provides participants with marketable abilities for their future

Basic Welding Certification Program

Our comprehensive welding program provides hands-on training in fundamental welding techniques that can lead to employment opportunities in various industries.

Course Curriculum:

  • Safety procedures and protective equipment usage
  • Introduction to welding tools and equipment
  • Arc welding (SMAW) fundamentals
  • MIG welding (GMAW) techniques
  • Reading and interpreting basic blueprints
  • Metal preparation and joint types
  • Weld inspection and quality control
  • Basic metal fabrication projects

Certificate of Completion

Upon successful completion, participants receive a recognized Basic Welding Certificate that demonstrates competency in fundamental welding techniques.

Enroll in Welding Program

  • Typography and text effects

  • Preparing images for web and print

Enroll in Photoshop Program

Basic Cake Baking & Decorating Program

This hands-on baking program teaches fundamental cake preparation and decoration techniques, providing skills for potential small business opportunities or employment in bakeries.

Course Curriculum:

  • Kitchen safety and sanitation practices
  • Understanding baking ingredients and measurements
  • Basic cake recipes (vanilla, chocolate, sponge)
  • Buttercream and frosting preparation
  • Cake leveling, filling, and crumb coating
  • Basic piping techniques and borders
  • Fondant handling and covering
  • Simple cake decoration designs
  • Packaging and presentation for sale
  • Costing and pricing for small business

Baking Arts Certificate

Participants who complete the program receive a Baking Arts Certificate acknowledging their proficiency in basic cake baking and decoration.

Enroll in Baking Program

Basic Sewing & Garment Repair Program

Programs for Severe Cases

Extended programs designed for participants struggling with substance abuse or severe behavioral issues

20 Days
Minimum

Substance Abuse Intervention Program

For participants with identified drug or alcohol abuse issues, we require a minimum 20-day program to establish foundation for recovery.

  • Detoxification supervision (if needed)
  • Individual and group counseling sessions
  • Substance abuse education
  • Relapse prevention planning
  • Introduction to support systems
2-6 Months
Recommended

Comprehensive Rehabilitation Program

For sustained recovery and behavioral change, we recommend programs of 2 to 6 months duration.

  • Extended therapeutic interventions
  • Multiple skills development certifications
  • Staged reintegration planning
  • Family therapy sessions
  • Aftercare program development
6-12 Months
Severe Cases

Extended Transformation Program

For the most severe cases involving long-term substance abuse or deeply entrenched behavioral issues.

  • Intensive psychological support
  • Multiple skills mastery certifications
  • Work readiness preparation
  • Community service projects
  • Gradual transition back to society

Note: Our program is designed to provide the time needed for genuine transformation and rehabilitation. Longer durations typically yield more sustainable results.
